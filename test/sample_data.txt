<h2>Copyright infringement notifications</h2>
<p>If you have reason to believe that any material or activity on a site controlled or operated by Creative Commons (such as <a href="https://creativecommons.org/">creativecommons.org</a> or <a href="https://wiki.creativecommons.org/">wiki.creativecommons.org</a> is infringing of the right(s) owned by you or someone else, for whom you have authority to act, please follow our <a href="https://creativecommons.org/dmca/">DMCA Notice &amp; Takedown Procedure</a>.</p>
<h2>Complaints about financial impropriety and other misconduct</h2>
<p>As a nonprofit entity, the integrity of CC’s financial information is paramount. We have adopted <a href="http://ibiblio.org/cccr/docs/conduct.pdf">Codes of Conduct</a> that prohibit financial impropriety and protect whistle blowers who bring such irregularities to our attention. Additionally, we take seriously other misconduct that may involve the violation of <a href="https://wiki.creativecommons.org/wiki/Public_reports#Policies_for_staff.2C_board_and_officers.2C_and_community_members">our policies</a> including, but not limited to, harassment.</p>
<p>If you are aware of any conduct prohibited by law or by our policies, you are encouraged to make a complaint, anonymously if you wish, to the members of the company’s Audit Committee. The email address for such complaints is <a href="mailto:audit@creativecommons.org">audit@creativecommons.org</a> which will forward your message automatically to the members of the Audit Committee. The Committee members are identified on our <a href="https://creativecommons.org/board">Board</a> page. You may also submit a complaint by post or fax to the attention of “Audit Committee” at our Mountain View address.</p>
<p>Creative Commons is a Massachusetts-chartered 501(c)(3) tax-exempt charitable corporation. For more information, see the corporate <a href="http://ibiblio.org/cccr/docs/articles.pdf">charter</a>, <a href="http://ibiblio.org/cccr/docs/bylaws.pdf">by-laws</a>, most recent <a href="http://ibiblio.org/cccr/docs/990.pdf">tax return</a> and most recent <a href="http://ibiblio.org/cccr/docs/audit.pdf">audited financial statement</a>.</p>
